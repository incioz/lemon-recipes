Rosemary and Lemon Roast Lamb

_________________________________________________________________________________

_________________________________________________________________________________

A different, but noncomplicated way of cooking lamb. Featuring a combination of lemon, rosemary, and mustard seeds for unique flavor.

Original recipe here.

_________________________________________________________________________________

Ingredients

  • 1 lamb leg boned
  • 3 garlic cloves minced
  • 3 tbs mustard seeds
  • 1 tbs fresh rosemary
  • 1 lemon slice, zested
  • 3 tbs oil
_________________________________________________________________________________

Directions

  1. Mix together mustard, garlic, rosemary, lemon zest and oil. Spoon over lamb and season.

  2. Place lemon slices in baking tray and lay lamb on top. Allow to sit for at least 20 minutes before roasting.

  3. Cook for approximately 1½ hours in a 200C oven, depending on weight.

  4. Cover with foil and allow to rest for 15-20 minutes before carving.
